### myLifeEcho
*Encrypted map of your digital life.*

> "myLifeEcho is not a vault of files. It is a vault of where your life lives."

**The Mission** While traditional password managers can pass on your online accounts, they cannot navigate the chaotic terabytes of data on your physical hard drives. **myLifeEcho** is a digital legacy navigator. It is designed to guide your trusted heirs directly to your most critical assets—crypto seeds, family archives, or legal documents—without uploading anything to the cloud.

**How It Works** Instead of duplicating gigabytes of data into a slow, massive container, myLifeEcho creates a lightweight, heavily encrypted "treasure map" of your file paths.

1. **Mark & Map:** You select the critical folders and files across your local drives. The system records their exact paths.
2. **Memory-Locked Encryption:** The map is encrypted using the **Memory Echo** protocol. The decryption key is derived from Q&A based on shared human memories, fortified by **Time-Lock** computational delay to prevent brute-force attacks.
3. **Dynamic Recovery:** In an emergency, your trusted heir answers the questions. The system reconstructs the key, decrypts the map, and reveals exactly where your life's work is stored on the physical machine.

**Key Advantages** * **Zero Data Duplication:** Secures access to terabytes of data by encrypting just the structural paths (a file of mere kilobytes).
* **Cloud-Free Legacy:** Your actual files never leave your device. 
* **Human-Centric Security:** Unlocked not by hardware tokens or 256-bit passwords written on paper, but by memories only you and your loved ones share.
