### myLifeEcho
*Encrypted map of your digital life.*

> "myLifeEcho is not a vault of files. It is a vault of where your life lives."

**The Mission**
While traditional password managers can pass on your online accounts, they cannot navigate the chaotic terabytes of data on your physical hard drives. **myLifeEcho** is a digital legacy navigator. It is designed to guide your trusted heirs directly to your most critical assets—crypto seeds, family archives, or legal documents—without uploading anything to the cloud.

**How It Works**
Instead of duplicating gigabytes of data into a slow, massive container, myLifeEcho creates a lightweight "treasure map" of your file paths, securely sealed within a single **`.psq`** container.

1. **Mark & Map:** You select the critical folders and files across your local drives. The system records their exact paths.
2. **Phantom-Step Cascade Encryption:** The map is encrypted into a `.psq` file using **Phantom-Step Cascade**—a proprietary cascade encryption method developed by the SecretMemoryLocker team. It transforms your personal memories into a dynamic security architecture with no equal in the world of cybersecurity. Most vaults simply lock the door. We ensure the next door doesn't even exist until you open the first one. At its core lies **Argon2id** — the global standard for breach-resistant hashing.
3. **Dynamic Recovery:** In an emergency, your trusted heir answers the Q&A based on shared human memories. The system reconstructs the key step-by-step, decrypts the `.psq` container, and reveals exactly where your life's work is stored on the physical machine.

**Key Advantages**
* **Zero Data Duplication:** Secures access to terabytes of data by encrypting just the structural paths (a `.psq` file of mere kilobytes).
* **Cloud-Free Legacy:** Your actual files never leave your device. 
* **Human-Centric Security:** Unlocked not by hardware tokens or 256-bit passwords written on paper, but by memories only you and your loved ones share.
